Put your training .mid files in this folder before running prepare_data.py.

You can get free MIDI datasets from sources such as:
- https://www.kaggle.com/datasets (search "classical piano midi")
- http://www.piano-midi.de/
- https://magenta.tensorflow.org/datasets/maestro

Aim for at least 20-50 MIDI files of a similar style (e.g. all classical
piano) for the model to learn a consistent musical pattern.
